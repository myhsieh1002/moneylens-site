MoneyLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

MoneyLens is a personal finance management app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Storage

All your financial data (accounts, transactions, categories, budgets, investments, insurance records) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) to your other devices. We do not have access to your iCloud data — it is encrypted and managed entirely by Apple.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or store your financial data on our servers.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.

AI Receipt Scanning

MoneyLens offers an optional AI-powered receipt scanning feature. When you use this feature:

  • Your receipt image is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for text extraction.
  • The image is processed in real-time and is not stored by Anthropic after processing.
  • If you provide your own Anthropic API key, the request is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's servers.
  • You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Subscriptions & Purchases

MoneyLens Pro subscriptions and lifetime purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Subscription management is handled by Apple — you can manage or cancel your subscription in your device's Settings.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We cannot access your iCloud-synced data.

API Key Storage

If you choose to enter your own Anthropic API key, it is stored securely in your device's Keychain (Apple's encrypted credential storage). It is never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

MoneyLens is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
