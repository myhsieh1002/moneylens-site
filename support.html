FAQ

How do I sync data across devices?

MoneyLens uses iCloud to sync your data automatically. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices and that iCloud is enabled for MoneyLens in Settings.

How do I use AI receipt scanning?

Tap the Import button, then choose Camera or Photo Library. MoneyLens will use AI to extract transaction details from your receipt. Free users get 5 scans per month; Pro users get 30.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You can change or cancel your MoneyLens Pro subscription at any time.

How do I export my data?

MoneyLens Pro users can export their ledger as a JSON file. Go to More > Export to create a backup.

I lost my data. Can I recover it?

If you have iCloud enabled, your data should sync automatically. If you previously exported a backup, you can restore it from More > Restore.
